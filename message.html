Clinical Alerts

Customize automatic messaging for each of your patients, ensuring that they take necessary medications on time and take proper care of their health. We make it possible for you to set reminders at a certain time of day or even every other week, and at any pattern depending on each patient's prescriptions or even daily actions they need to do to maintain their health. Not only does this ensure the conditions of your patients, but it increases patient satisfaction.

Manage

Our automatic messaging service can be customized to communicate with employees, sending individual, customized messages to staff members for shift reminders, emergency alerts, opportunities to fill open shifts. Not only does this help organize things for your patients, but it also does so for the people working in your practice, ensuring that everything runs smoothly for in-person staff/doctors and those who work virtually.
