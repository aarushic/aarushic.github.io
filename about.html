About ConnectHealth

About Us

ConnectHealth is a medical communications company based in Dallas dedicated to providing our customers with easy to use and effective tools to communicate with their patients. We are here to streamline communication between doctors and their patients and provide a direct line of contact to make medical appointments and have constant advice from your healthcare providers. All while not loosing track of any of your medical records.
