Direct Chat Line

Help your patients connect directly to an on duty staff member that will help guide them.

Benefits

There are many benefits to our direct chat line service. The ability to exclusively connect to a guide or a person there to always make sure your patients have their time accounted for, questions answered, and purpose fulfilled is something your practice will greatly benefit from.

More

Benefit #1

Allows you and your patient to establish a direct line of communication while they navigate your website and digital features.

Benefit #2

Lets your patient easily resolve any problems that may come up during the online process by allowing them to communicate with an actual person.

Benefit #3

Comes with a feature that will display if someone is able to talk with the patient, how many people are ahead of them in line, and the estimated time it will take.
