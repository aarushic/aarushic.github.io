RECYCLING COMPETITION

Listen to our experts talk about and further your knowledge on climate change.

Why?

Plastic, Paper, Cardboard, and Glass, some of the most used materials in the world, are recyclable. Although only around 9% of recycled materials get recycled. In addition, the municipal solid waste recycling rate in the US is as low as 35%. Considering how 75% of American waste is recyclable, it's even more astonishing. Many things contribute to these low numbers - from how we manage our waste systems to the unavailability of recycling. This has to change, as recycling helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions by reducing energy consumption.

How

To raise awareness, we’ve come up with a fun way to recycle. By recycling materials into art! Gather all the recyclable materials you can find, and create whatever your imagination provides you. You can use plastic bottles, cardboard, glass, even paper bags. Work in teams, individually, in a large-scale project, or even something that takes you 5 minutes to complete, as long as you have fun! To make this even more fun, post it on TikTok, and gather views. Just remember to add our hashtag and post within the time frame of the event! May the most viewed TikTok, aka, best recycler win!
