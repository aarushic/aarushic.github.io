Meet Our Speakers

Ioan M Magee

Born in Bulgaria, Ioan has been passionate about the effects of climate change since a young age. Ioan is the founder of Bulgaria For Change, a now nationally known non-profit organization seeking to increase awareness of climate change in his country and the world. He is known for helping youths in lesser-known communities have a greater voice in this fight for sustainability and advocating for green policies.

Osbeorn I Howard

Originally from Greater Manchester, England, Osbeorn comes from a long generation of people fighting against climate change, more specifically deforestation and its effects on the ecosystem. Specializing in the field of ecology, Howard has made significant contributions in the field. Speaking at the UN Climate action conference, and being the co-founder of Earth Comes First, a non-profit organization raising awareness of the effect of deforestation and climate change on more than just humans, are two of his long list of accomplishments.

Marta K Bello

A Dutch advocate and an entrepreneur, Marta began her journey by exploring the field of Energy and Sustainability. Starting by being an environmental engineer, Marta has made significant contributions in the field. With her knowledge of the power of harnessing solar energy, she founded Sun and Co., a non-profit organization working on implementing solar power in less accessible areas and making it ideal for people of all standings.

Esteve O Amhalghaidh

A leading environmental expert from Catalonia, Spain, Esteve began his journey in climate change after experiencing the first hand effects of droughts and increased temperatures. As the founder of Youth & Harvest , Esteve is using the voice of the youth to help bring about change for the people who cannot.

Green Awakening

Green Awakening is a climate conference dedicated to teaching others about the devastating effects of climate change and what we can do to combat it. Our mission is to ensure that our planet will be habitable for our future generations.
